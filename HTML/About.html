About

Welcome to the University Of Kelaniya Computer Club! We are a dynamic community of tech enthusiasts, innovators, and problem solvers dedicated to exploring the exciting world of computer science and technology. Founded in 2006, our club has been a hub for students passionate about coding, robotics, and all things tech-related. Our mission is to foster a collaborative and creative environment where students can expand their knowledge, gain hands-on experience, and connect with like-minded peers. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced coder seeking new challenges, we have something to offer you. Join us in our journey to explore, create, and innovate in the ever-evolving realm of technology.
